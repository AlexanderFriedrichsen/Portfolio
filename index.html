About Me

Alex Friedrichsen is a data scientist specializing in business intelligence and machine learning, and a professional Magic: The Gathering player for Team Handshake.

Bio

Alex Friedrichsen is a data scientist who specializes in business intelligence and machine learning, with expertise in building automated data pipelines and predictive analytics solutions. He holds a master's degree in Data Science from the University of Vermont's Complex Systems Center, where his research focused on evolutionary computation and network analysis. Currently, Alex leads strategy for Team Handshake as a professional Magic: The Gathering player, bringing his analytical mindset to both competitive gaming and data science.

Beyond his technical work, Alex is an active member of Vermont's music scene, playing jazz piano with his band The Kyne. When he's not analyzing data or competing in Magic tournaments, you can find him working on climbing problems at local crags or automating daily tasks with Python scripts. His approach to both work and play is guided by a passion for optimization and strategic thinking.
