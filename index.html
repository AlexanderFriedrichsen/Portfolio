Hi! I'm Alex Friedrichsen. I graduated the University of Vermont with my Master's degree in Data Science and Complex Systems. I graduated with my bachelor's degree in Data Science in the spring of 2022 through the Honors College at UVM while taking accelerated master's classes. I worked as a software engineer II at BAE Systems for the past year. Many of my responsibilities were in line with an analytics role, however, as I worked to create BI dashboards. Prior to my role at BAE, I did research in multiple labs at UVM including the Social-Ecological Gaming and Simulation (SEGS) lab, the Computational Ethics lab, and the GooglexUVM Open-Source Projects and Communities research team.

Some of my interests include game theory, music, agent-based models, evolutionary computation, philosophy of mind and chaos theory! I enjoy both technical and holistic learning experiences. I am a huge proponent of habits and routine to promote daily flexibility through self-automation. I love to read about self-improvement, behavioral economics, theory versus action, and fantasy! I try to optimize and strategize everything.

I am a professional Magic: The Gathering player with one pro tour top 8. I love to exercise; I rock climb, run, swim, bike, and play a mean game of Spikeball. I am an avid board game player poker player. I also like to play jazz piano in my band (on Instagram @thekyne.band), make lists, and automate random things with scripts!
